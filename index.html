About Me

I began my coding journey in October 2022, with no prior experience, and a year after COVID dashed my (in hindsight, ill-advised) childhood dreams of majoring in musical theatre out-of-state. In that year following my high school graduation, I worked two dead-end customer service jobs and found myself craving to do and be so much more.

I’ve always had a passion for helping people, but I knew there had to be a better way for me to do that on a much broader scale than slinging coffees as a barista or taking calls as a customer service representative for the rest of my days. After a great deal of research, I came to the conclusion that, in our ever-increasingly tech-dominated world, there’s just no better way to improve the lives of the every-man than taking up a career in coding.
